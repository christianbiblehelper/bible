The Beginning

  Our very first reading regards the start of the bible. So, genesis was a beginning for us in many ways. Hopefully it may be a beginning for our viewers: to have love enter their world.
In the series I am about to start, I hope to welcome people to a place of comfort.

   Yes, a place of comfort, in its truest sense. To reach both these virtues, true and the 'comfortable', I will seek to write and talk by striking to the core and by being true.
I wish to find what's best for well-being, the spiritual journey and you the audience in particular. I wish you and myself welcome to this journey. It is of mine, it is of yours.
