The Title

Many a text begins in a certain way. You should start with an introduction, follow with a body, and end with a conclusion.

The Beginning

The body of any text should always contain the bulk of information. The introduced point should be backed up with arguments. Even the body of fiction should be a device linking back to the beginning. Namely, without that part it is not a whole.
